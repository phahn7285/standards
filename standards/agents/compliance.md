\# compliance Standards



\## Standards

\- Compliance requirements MUST be identified, documented, and enforced.

\- Regulatory obligations MUST be mapped to technical controls.

\- Compliance controls MUST be auditable and evidence-backed.

\- Compliance requirements MUST NOT be implemented ad hoc.

\- Controls MUST be enforceable via automation where feasible.

\- Exceptions MUST follow agents/change-management.md.

\- Compliance posture MUST be continuously assessed.

\- Compliance ownership MUST be explicit.

\- Compliance reporting MUST be reproducible.

\- Compliance rules MUST align with agents/security.md and agents/privacy.md.



\## Scope

In scope:

\- Regulatory and contractual compliance

\- Control mapping and enforcement

\- Audit readiness and evidence generation

\- Policy adherence verification



Out of scope:

\- Legal interpretation of regulations

\- Business policy authoring



Assumptions:

\- Regulated environments may exist.

\- Compliance may vary by region and industry.



\## Responsibilities

\- Compliance owners:

&nbsp; - Define required controls

&nbsp; - Validate adherence



\- Engineering:

&nbsp; - Implement technical controls

&nbsp; - Produce audit evidence



\- Reviewers:

&nbsp; - Validate compliance coverage



\## Usage

\- Apply this file when:

&nbsp; - regulatory or contractual obligations exist

&nbsp; - handling sensitive or regulated data

\- This file MUST be composed with:

&nbsp; - agents/security.md

&nbsp; - agents/privacy.md

&nbsp; - agents/devsecops.md

&nbsp; - agents/orchestration.md



\## Structure

\- Controls MUST be traceable to requirements.

\- Evidence MUST be reproducible from systems.



\## File Decomposition

\- Requirements:

&nbsp; - MUST define applicable regulations.

\- Controls:

&nbsp; - MUST define enforcement mechanisms.

\- Evidence:

&nbsp; - MUST define validation artifacts.



\## Application Rules

\- Compliance MUST be enforced by default.

\- Gaps MUST trigger remediation.

\- Audits MUST be supported without manual intervention.

\- Violations MUST be treated as defects.
